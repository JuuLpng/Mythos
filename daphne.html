Daphne Nora Delgado

A darker slice-of-life with surreal twists

What to Expect

Daphne Nora Delgado (D.N.D.) follows the quietly unraveling life of a girl caught between the mundane and the mysterious. This slice-of-life tale explores the fragile boundary between grief, growth, and the hidden truths that shadow our most ordinary days.

With a tone that leans into somber introspection, surreal interruptions, and subtle magical realism, D.N.D. invites you into a slow-burning narrative about isolation, memory, and resilience in the face of subtle yet profound change.

This is the first iteration of Daphne Nora Delgado
