About MythosWorks Inc.

Our Mission

At MythosWorks Inc., our mission is to break the boundaries of creativity and technology, making anything possible. We strive to inspire innovation and push the limits of what's achievable through our products and projects. Whether it's in the realm of gaming, app development, or entertainment, we believe in creating immersive experiences that connect people and bring new possibilities to life. Our dedication is to empower individuals and communities to explore their imagination, learn, and grow in a world where the only limit is the extent of their dreams.

Why We Started?

My colleagues and I founded MythosWorks Inc. with the belief that imagination should never be limited by cost or dismissed as impossible. Too often, innovative ideas are overlooked by major companies and investors who don’t share the vision of the everyday creator. We believe that accessibility, community, and creativity should drive innovation. That’s why MythosWorks Inc. is dedicated to being a company built for the people, by the people—where ideas are heard, possibilities are explored, and imagination becomes reality.
